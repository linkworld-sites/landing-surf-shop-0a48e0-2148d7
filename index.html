How It Works

Renting Is Ridiculously Easy

Get in the water fast. Our streamlined rental process means less time on dry land and more time on waves.

1
Pick Your Board

Tell us your skill level, the break you're heading to, and your height/weight. We'll match you with the perfect board.

2
Add Your Gear

Grab a wetsuit, leash, fins, and wax. Everything you need is here — we'll even wax the board for you.

3
Hit the Waves

Head to the break and surf! Daily, weekly, or multi-day rates available. Return anytime before close.

4
Return & Repeat

Rinse off the board, drop it back with us. Zero damage fees for normal surf wear. See you tomorrow! 🤙
